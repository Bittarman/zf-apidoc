\Zend_Pdf_Cmap_ByteEncoding_Static
jump to top

Package: Zend\Pdf\Fonts

Custom cmap type used for the Adobe Standard 14 PDF fonts.

Just like {@link Zend_Pdf_Cmap_ByteEncoding} except that the constructor takes a predefined array of glyph numbers and can cover any Unicode character.
